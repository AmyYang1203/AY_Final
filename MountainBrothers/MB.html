The group
attended the
Pennsylvania
State University
in State College
Pennsylvania.

They produced
national radio
and TV commercials
for Sprite on the ad
"Rhymes from
The Mind".

Triple Crown was
the first album they
made that was
considered to be
an independent
hip-hop classic.

They are named after
the legend of 108
mountain bandits in the
Chinese novel Water Margin

They are known as cultural
pioneers for being one of the
first Asian-American hip-hop
groups and lack of sampling
of other music in their tracks.

They would not send
in pictures of
themselves to record
labels in order to
prevent prejudice
that might come
with being Asian.s
